Driving Personality Quiz

This project was inspired by the purchase of my first car back in September.

I love to drive and couldn't wait until I was old enough to get my permit. My family says I'm an aggressive driver, which is far from true.

I made this quiz to test that accusation and through the use of "If statements" each input creates an output generating what type of driver you are.
